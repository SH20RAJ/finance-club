# Finance Club BIT Mesra (FinPro / FINWHIZ) - Research Report

## 1. Overview
The Finance Club at BIT Mesra, often referred to as **FinPro** or **FINWHIZ**, is a student-run technical club dedicated to fostering financial literacy and economic awareness among students.

**Mission & Vision:**
- To familiarize students with the economic and financial sector, terms, and concepts.
- To bridge the gap between financial theory and practical application.
- To equip students with skills for managing personal finances, generating passive income, and understanding the corporate financial world.
- To enhance employability in domains like Banking, Insurance, Equities, and Derivatives.

## 3. Activities & Offerings
The club organizes a wide range of activities to engage students in finance:

### Educational Initiatives
- **Financial Management Course:** A ~30-hour non-credit course covering financial fundamentals and software training.
- **Lectures & Seminars:** Sessions on GST, Taxation, Insurance, Investment options, and Mutual Funds.
- **Live Sessions:** Interactive discussions with industry experts.

### Competitions & Forums
- **Budget Eye:** A forum for analyzing and discussing the Union Budget with professors.
- **The Case Folio:** Analysis of current financial issues and case studies.
- **Market Watch:** Analysis and critique of business news and management issues.
- **Stock Market Simulation:** A virtual trading game where students invest mock money based on real-time market data (Sensex/Nifty) to learn trading strategies without financial risk.
- **Mergers & Acquisitions Role-Playing:** Simulations of corporate takeovers and deal structuring.
